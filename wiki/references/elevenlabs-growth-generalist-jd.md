## Job Description (verbatim)

> **About ElevenLabs**
>
> ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.
>
> We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - **multiples of 11, always**.
>
> We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:
>
> **ElevenAgents** enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
>
> **ElevenCreative** empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
>
> **ElevenAPI** gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.
>
> Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. **We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders.** If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.
>
> **How we work**
>
> **High-velocity:** Rapid experimentation, lean autonomous teams, and minimal bureaucracy.
>
> **Impact not job titles:** We don't have job titles. Instead, it's about the impact you have. No task is above or beneath you.
>
> **AI first:** We use AI to move faster with higher-quality results. We do this across the whole company—from engineering to growth to operations.
>
> **Excellence everywhere:** Everything we do should match the quality of our AI models.
>
> **Global team:** We prioritize your talent, not your location.
>
> **What we offer**
>
> **Innovative culture:** You'll be part of a generational opportunity to define the trajectory of AI, surrounded by a team pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
>
> **Growth paths:** Joining ElevenLabs means joining a dynamic team with countless opportunities to drive impact - beyond your immediate role and responsibilities.
>
> **Learning & development:** ElevenLabs proactively supports professional development through an annual discretionary stipend.
>
> **Social travel:** We also provide an annual discretionary stipend to meet up with colleagues each year, however you choose.
>
> **Annual company offsite:** Each year, we bring the entire team together in a new location - past offsites have included Croatia and Italy.
>
> **Co-working:** If you're not located near one of our main hubs, we offer a monthly co-working stipend.
>
> **About the role**
>
> We are looking for a high performing generalist to join our B2B Marketing team. You will join us in turning tens of millions of marketing spend into hundreds of millions of pipeline. In this role, you will surge on the highest priority projects, and help fill the gaps as needed. In a single day, you might expect to negotiate billboard inventory in SFO airport, craft the talking points for a keynote speech for a crowd of thousands, or build a report to analyze the efficacy of our influencer marketing. We don't care if you have previous B2B marketing experience - so long as you are passionate about what we do, are willing to work hard, and might be described by your friends as one of the smartest people they know.
>
> **Requirements**
>
> Great copywriting skills
>
> Extremely organized and able to keep track of multiple projects while constantly context switching
>
> Capable of writing a SQL query, creating a dashboard, and building a spreadsheet model
>
> Able to learn new tools and software quickly
>
> Creative
>
> Passionate about ElevenLabs' products
>
> This role is remote and can be executed globally. If you prefer, you can work from our offices in Bangalore, Dublin, London, New York, San Francisco, Tokyo, and Warsaw.

**New signals locked in from verbatim (vs structured summary):**

- "**IOI medalists and ex-founders**" — explicit talent-bar signal. Competitive-programming AND founder-shape, not corporate-marketer-shape. Match Mark's [[concepts/founding-gtm-engineer-thesis|engineer-seller framing]], not demand-gen-funnel framing.
- "**Past offsites have included Croatia and Italy**" — European-friendly culture signal; reduces Vienna-base concern.
- "**Builders doing the best work of their lives**" — operating-culture self-image; the role-shape language is "builder," not "marketer."
- "**Might be described by your friends as one of the smartest people they know**" — explicit talent-bar signal in plain English. This is the requirement under all the SQL/copy/multitask checkboxes.

## What this role actually is

Weighted reading of the JD (the JD itself is intentionally short, so weights are inferred from the verbs and the team placement):

| Pillar | Weight | What it actually is |
|---|---|---|
| Demand-gen + paid execution | ~30% | Owning execution surface area across paid channels (OOH like airport billboards, digital, influencer). The "tens of millions of marketing spend into hundreds of millions of pipeline" framing puts pipeline-attribution math at the centre. |
| Marketing analytics + reporting | ~25% | SQL queries, dashboards, spreadsheet modelling. Pipeline efficacy and channel attribution are the load-bearing analytical outputs. ^[inferred] |
| Copywriting + content production | ~20% | Keynote talking points called out explicitly; the broader B2B content surface (case studies, launch copy, ad creative) likely sits here too. ^[inferred] |
| Event / field marketing + sponsorships | ~15% | Billboard inventory negotiation is one example; the wider category is field, conferences, sponsorships, and any high-touch surface that requires deal-making with external vendors. ^[inferred] |
| Misc generalist projects | ~10% | Whatever else the B2B Marketing team needs filled in a high-velocity / no-titles environment. |

Effective role: **operator-generalist on a B2B Marketing team at a frontier-lab AI company**, with pipeline-attribution math as the through-line under a high-variance project surface. The "no prior B2B marketing experience required" line plus the requirements list (SQL, modelling, copywriting, multi-project ownership) signals they are hiring for **operator shape, not marketing pedigree**.

## Fit notes for Mark

**Strong:**
- **Operator shape matches.** The seat asks for a multi-project owner who can do SQL, build dashboards, model in spreadsheets, write copy, and learn new tools fast. This is the founding-GTM shape Mark has been running for ~2.5 years at Stackgini ([[entities/stackgini]]) — three flagship AI sales tools built end-to-end ([[skills/ai-powered-sales-tooling]]) are direct evidence of "quick learner for new tools" and "SQL + modelling" at a real bar.
- **"No formal titles, focus on impact"** maps onto Mark's existing seat, where the founding-GTM remit covers everything from ABM data plumbing to demo decks to fundraising support. The transition cost from "Stackgini founding GTM" to "ElevenLabs generalist" is structurally low; the scale of the marketing budget is the only step-change.
- **AI-first culture** ([[concepts/founding-gtm-engineer-thesis]]) is the explicit operating norm. ElevenLabs is one of a small number of companies where the engineer-seller thesis is priced in by default, not a thing to argue for in a cover letter.
- **Copywriting + analytical depth in one person** is the engineer-seller combination ([[skills/ai-powered-sales-tooling]] is half writing, half SQL/scripts). Most candidates are one or the other.
- **No B2B marketing background required, but Mark has one.** Mark sits above the floor on the explicitly-disclaimed requirement, and the founding-GTM work at Stackgini already covers the marketing wing (positioning, content, demand-gen tooling) in addition to sales.
- **Productization affinity** ([[user_productization_affinity]]) — the "convert spend into pipeline" framing is the bespoke-to-systems shape: the seat will reward whoever turns ad-hoc channel experiments into repeatable pipeline machines.

**Stretch:**
- **Scale step-change.** Stackgini's marketing spend is presumably small; ElevenLabs is operating tens-of-millions. Channel mix at that scale (OOH, large paid digital, sponsorships, influencer at the upper end of the funnel) is not something Mark has run dollars on. The seat would have him learning the playbook in real time.
- **Location.** Hubs are Dublin / London / New York / Bangalore / SF / Tokyo / Warsaw. **No Vienna.** Listing says remote-globally is fine, but B2B Marketing teams tend to cluster geographically for the high-touch event/field work. Remote-from-Vienna may be possible but is a question to surface explicitly in any application or screen. ^[ambiguous]
- **Comp not posted.** ElevenLabs at $11B is a hot-cap-table seat; cash comp may or may not be competitive with Vienna AE ceilings, and the real upside is equity, which depends on grant size and valuation step-ups. Worth asking on screen if pursued.
- **Bar is unusually high.** $781M raised, A16Z + ICONIQ + Sequoia backers, public profile as one of the most prestigious AI companies of the cohort. Applicant pool will include senior B2B marketing operators willing to take a "Generalist" title. The "no titles" framing cuts both ways: a 10-year demand-gen lead reading this JD also sees no friction.

**Calibration** (per [[synthesis/mark-progression-over-direction]]): ~90% commercial / ~10% off — the seat is fully on the GTM / marketing wing of commercial work. Same axis as the Vendure PMM seat ([[references/vendure-product-marketing-gtm-vienna-jd]]) but at a frontier-lab AI company rather than an OSS commerce framework. This is the **cleanest "frontier-lab GTM" instance** of [[synthesis/mark-job-direction-map]] tracked so far. Progression-wise, the seat is a meaningful tier-up on company prestige and budget scale while staying on the same commercial axis Mark is already operating on.

## Application angle if pursued

Differentiator vs typical "Growth Generalist" applicants (consultants pivoting in, BD/marketing operators with 3-5 years of pedigreed B2B work):

1. **Engineer-seller proof, not claim.** [[skills/ai-powered-sales-tooling]] is three production tools (ABM data layer, account research, cold-email engine) built by Mark on raw LLM APIs. ElevenLabs' "AI-first across growth and operations" line is exactly the shape this work demonstrates.
2. **Founding-GTM ownership at a B2B SaaS** ([[entities/stackgini]]) gives Mark cross-pillar fluency (positioning, demand-gen, sales-cycle ownership, fundraising-support) that few "Growth Generalist" candidates carry as a single operator. Stackgini intra-IT buying-centre work ([[feedback_stackgini_buying_centers]]) is concrete evidence of multi-stakeholder cycle management.
3. **Pipeline math literacy.** Luther HGB Excel + CBS Financial Management background ([[references/mark-education]]) gives Mark the modelling layer the seat explicitly asks for. Most marketing-pedigreed applicants do not carry this depth.
4. **Voice / audio AI angle.** Specific product-mastery beat to develop: ElevenLabs sells voice AI to enterprises; the buyer is a B2B IT / product / customer-experience function. Mark's Stackgini IT-buying-centre work maps directly onto how ElevenAgents lands in target accounts — credible enterprise-buyer instinct, not marketing-only instinct.
5. **Why-ElevenLabs narrative.** Need a non-generic "why frontier voice AI, why now, why ElevenLabs over Anthropic / OpenAI GTM seats." Possible angles: (a) the voice-AI surface is the part of the frontier where B2B procurement and consumer creative collide, an unusual GTM-shape; (b) the $781M / no-marketing-budget-discipline operating culture pairs unusually well with engineer-seller execution; (c) Mark's existing AI-tooling work compounds inside a company that already operates AI-first.

**Watch-outs:**
- Don't pitch this as a stepping stone — culture rewards generalists who *want* the operator shape, not those treating it as launchpad.
- Don't lead with "founding GTM" framing in a way that signals a desire for tier-up to "Head of X." The seat is impact-not-title; framing must match.
