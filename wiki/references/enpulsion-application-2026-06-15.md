## Tailoring decisions visible in this snapshot

What this ENPULSION CV does differently than the [[references/elevenlabs-application-2026-06-13|ElevenLabs snapshot]] and the [[references/vendure-application-2026-06-03|Vendure snapshot]]:

**Stackgini bullets restructured for the CoS seat (6 bullets, rewritten):**

- **Bullet 1 (scale + GTM-build proof)**: kept the $50K → $1M+ ARR / 3x YoY headline (most CoS applicants from consulting backgrounds have no comparable scale claim). Trimmed the operating-detail tail to leave room for the new bullets below; the *built the function from scratch* signal remains intact.
- **Bullet 2 — NEW, the direct JD echo**: "Ran quarterly planning, forecast, and pipeline-review cadences with the CEO and CRO; translated leadership decisions into action plans with owners and timelines." This bullet exists almost entirely as JD mirror copy. The JD line *Translate discussions and decisions into clear action plans, owners, and timelines* is the most concrete responsibility named in the posting; mirroring it in the candidate's own operating history is the single highest-leverage tailoring move in this CV.
- **Bullet 3 — AI tooling as productization, not as sales output**: same AI tooling fact as in prior snapshots, but reframed away from sales metrics ("$N net-new pipeline") and toward the *building lightweight tooling* / *systems that improve execution* framing. Maps onto the JD pillar *Build lightweight processes and systems that improve execution and communication*. The productization-affinity signal ([[user_productization_affinity]]) is doing the load-bearing work here.
- **Bullet 4 — CS pivot reframed as cross-functional**: "led the cross-functional pivot of customer success to a professional-services model" — the *cross-functional* qualifier is the JD echo (JD: *Coordinate stakeholders across functions and international locations*). Same underlying fact as in the ElevenLabs CV; different reading frame.
- **Bullet 5 — NEW, fundraising-support reps**: explicit founder-side capital-formation reps (investor sourcing, decks, data room, DDQ&A, customer reference calls, cap-table, term-sheet review). This is the *Support high-impact projects on behalf of the CEO* pillar, in compressed form, already done. Atypical for a CoS candidate; differentiator vs consulting-CoS applicants.
- **Bullet 6 — NEW, C/VP audience and decision materials**: "Pitched and managed C- and VP-level audiences at Siemens AG, SAP SE, Carl Zeiss AG, Danaher (F500), and DHL: produced decks and decision materials; ran workshops and scoped cross-functional problems." Maps onto the JD's twice-named *advanced PowerPoint and presentation skills* / *create clear, structured presentations and decision materials for leadership and stakeholder meetings* pillars. Reframes Mark's enterprise-customer pitching as exec-audience deck-craft, which is what the JD is recruiting for.

**Commercial-pillar bullets pruned:**

- **Enterprise sales metrics dropped** (25 accounts, 30% win rate, $50K ACV, 6-month avg cycle, named verticals). Present in all prior snapshots (Anthropic, Vendure, ElevenLabs); removed here because the ENPULSION JD has *no commercial pillar at all* — sales, marketing, customer-facing work are entirely absent from the posting. Sales metrics would compete for page real estate and read as misalignment with the seat shape.
- **Partner channel build dropped** (SAP LeanIX, GBTEC, Matrix42 co-sell mechanics). Same reason — the F500-via-channel signal does not earn its line on a CoS CV.
- **F500 customer sourcing dropped** as a standalone bullet. Customer-facing scale is preserved, but as audience-pitching evidence (bullet 6) rather than as commercial-win evidence.

**Other moves:**

- **"VC-backed" descriptor restored** on the Stackgini description line. (Dropped on Vendure CV; restored on ElevenLabs CV; kept on ENPULSION CV.) ENPULSION is itself a scaleup with capital-formation activity ahead; the VC-backed signal matches the stage frame.
- **Crypto interest dropped**. Same move as on the ElevenLabs CV. Crypto / on-chain finance does not fit ENPULSION's space-tech / hardware profile and would read as off-frame for the audience.
- **Education / volunteering / projects unchanged** from prior snapshots. CBS Cologne B.A. clears the JD's *BSc or equivalent in Business, Economics, Engineering* filter. TU Berlin CS reinforces the engineer-operator framing. Tendenz kept generic (full-stack-build framing) — same calibration as on Vendure.
- **No title change**. "Founding GTM Lead" is the title on the CV; the CoS pitch is built from the *content* of the bullets, not from rebranding the seat as something it isn't. The cover letter (when drafted) will need to carry the explicit *founding-GTM-as-internal-CoS-analogue* translation.

## What this CV deliberately does NOT do

Several moves were available and explicitly not taken:

- **No invented domain expertise**. The CV does not claim space-tech, propulsion, hardware, aerospace, or NewSpace reps Mark does not have. The *why-ENPULSION, why-space* answer is reserved for the cover letter and screen — not faked in the CV.
- **No PowerPoint name-drop**. The JD names *advanced PowerPoint* explicitly, but Mark works in Typst / Slides / Pitch / Figma, not PowerPoint. The CV claims *decks and decision materials* (true) without specifying the tool. Tool-specific framing held for the screen if asked.
- **No CoS / Chief of Staff title rebrand**. The seat at Stackgini is what it is. Restating it as quasi-CoS on the CV would invite skepticism; the bullet structure does the translation work instead.
- **No invented cross-functional ENPULSION mapping**. The intra-IT buying-center reps from Stackgini ([[feedback_stackgini_buying_centers]]) — the strongest internal analogue to the CoS connector role — are not name-checked on the CV. Reserved for the cover letter / screen where they can be unpacked.
- **No name-drop of the productization affinity** ([[user_productization_affinity]]) by name. The signal is carried by bullet 3 (lightweight AI tooling as systems-building) without being labeled.

The choice across these holds: lead on **scale + execution-translation + productization + founder-side reps**; let the cover letter carry **why-ENPULSION + why-space + the explicit CoS-shape pitch**.
