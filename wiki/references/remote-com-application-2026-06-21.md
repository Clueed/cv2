- **No consulting pedigree implied**. The CV does not dress the founding-operator background as strategy-house experience; it leans on the JD's explicit OR-branch instead. - **No EOR / HR-tech domain claims**. Mark has zero EOR reps; the CV stays on the transferable B2B-SaaS market-dynamics ground and does not fabricate global-employment expertise.
- **No C-level-at-scale claim**. The exec-partnering bullet says CEO/CRO of a scale-up (true), not C-suite-of-a-large-org (untrue).
- **No demand-gen / funnel-jargon padding**. Kept dense and strategy-shaped per voice rules.
- **No take-home-case preview on the CV**. The case is the screen; the CV's job is to earn it.
