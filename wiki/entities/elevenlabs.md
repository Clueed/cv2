## Products

### ElevenAgents — enterprise voice + chat agents (B2B core)

Platform for deploying voice and chat agents at scale, with integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability. **Conversational AI 2.0** launched 2025/2026 with state-of-the-art turn-taking model (handles hesitations, filler words, interruptions in real-time). Multimodal (text + voice + image). This is the centre of gravity for B2B GTM.

**Named enterprise deployments:**
- **Revolut** (Jan 2026) — customer support across UK + Europe, 4M+ customers, 8x faster time-to-resolution across 30+ languages.
- **Klarna** (Feb 2026) — first-line phone support for 35M US customers, up to 10x faster resolutions.
- **Razorpay** (India) — outbound merchant calls in Hinglish, structured-insight capture per conversation.
- **Deutsche Telekom** — Magenta AI Call Assistant embedded in network infrastructure (unveiled at MWC 2026, rolling out across Germany, planned 50-language support over 12 months).
- **Customers Bank** (Jun 2026) — strategic collaboration for customer service + internal operations.
- **IBM** (Mar 2026) — TTS + STT integrated into watsonx Orchestrate agentic AI platform.
- **Harvey** — listed alongside Deutsche Telekom and Meta as flagship enterprise customer.
- **Salesforce, Square, Ukrainian Government** — among publicly cited customers.

### ElevenCreative — creator and marketer tooling

Speech, music, image, and video generation across **70+ languages, 5,000+ voices**. **Eleven v3** is the company's expressive offline TTS model leading on language coverage and expressiveness. Includes a creator marketplace (added Matthew McConaughey as investor/voice). Also a separate music marketplace (ElevenMusic). Has a separate B2B Marketing Lead role dedicated to ElevenCreative ([[references/elevenlabs-growth-generalist-jd|see sibling JDs]]).

### ElevenAPI — developer access to foundational audio models

Speed-optimized variants like **Flash v2.5** (real-time agent class). API + SDK surface. Powers everything else.

## Operating model

ElevenLabs **eliminated all job titles** ~mid-2025. CEO Mati publicly confirms the experiment "is working well." Core principles ([[concepts/elevenlabs-no-titles-operating-model]]):

- **Impact, not job titles** — no formal titles; no task above or beneath anyone.
- **High-velocity** — rapid experimentation, lean autonomous teams, minimal bureaucracy.
- **AI-first** — AI used across engineering, growth, and operations to move faster with higher-quality results.
- **Excellence everywhere** — work matches the quality bar of the AI models.
- **Global team** — talent prioritized over location.
- **Org shape**: ~20 micro-teams of 5-10 people.
- **Bar signal**: "IOI medalists and ex-founders" referenced in JD body as part of the team profile — the talent bar is competitive-programming and founder-shape, not corporate-marketer-shape.
- **Culture quirk**: "multiples of 11, always" — internal numerology around the company's name (e.g. revenue/headcount/comms targets are framed in 11-multiples).

## Competitive position (voice AI, 2026)

The 2026 TTS / voice-agent market is **use-case-driven, not winner-take-all**, with three tiers (per [Chaos and Order, May 2026](https://www.youngju.dev/blog/culture/2026-05-14-ai-voice-2026-elevenlabs-openai-realtime-cartesia-vapi-sesame-deepgram-comparison-deep-dive.en)):

| Tier | Use case | Leaders |
|---|---|---|
| Expressive offline | High-fidelity narration, dubbing, content | **ElevenLabs Eleven v3**, Inworld Realtime, Hume Octave |
| Real-time agent | Low-latency conversational voice agents | ElevenLabs Flash v2.5, **Cartesia Sonic 3**, Deepgram Aura-2, Rime Coda |
| High-volume cheap | Bulk/low-cost TTS | Vapi Voices Beta ($0.0025/min), Polly Standard, Azure Neural |

**Direct competitors:**

- **Cartesia** — raised $100M late 2025; Sonic-3 (Apr 2026), Sonic-3.5 (May 2026). Fastest TTS at ~75ms time-to-first-byte. **Decisively ahead in low-latency voice-agent scenarios.**
- **Hume AI** — Hume Octave 2 leads emotional fidelity; EVI 4 conversational. Differentiated on emotion recognition + generation.
- **OpenAI Realtime API** — full-stack player; the "default safe choice" for builders already on OpenAI.
- **Sesame** — newer expressive voice player.
- **Deepgram** — STT-first, expanding into Aura-2 TTS for real-time agents.
- **Suno / Udio** — music generation (ElevenMusic competes here).
- **Vapi** — orchestration layer for voice agents; uses multiple TTS providers.

**ElevenLabs' positioning**: category leader in **consumer TTS + B2B voice cloning + creative production**, climbing up the stack into **B2B voice agents**. Highest revenue, broadest product surface, deepest enterprise list.

**Risks**: Cartesia's latency lead in agent scenarios is real; OpenAI's default-platform pull is structural; legal exposure on celebrity voices (Taylor Swift lawsuit referenced).
