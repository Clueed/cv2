# Remote (remote.com)

**What it is**: Global-employment platform solving compliant international hiring. Remote handles the EOR (Employer of Record), payroll, benefits, and compliance layer so companies of any size can recruit, pay, and manage teams across borders. Positions itself as building a "best-in-class HR platform."

**Operating model**: Fully remote (every position), async-first ("plan your schedule around your life, not around meetings"), globally distributed workforce across 6 continents. Core values include Innovation; the company has explicitly baked **Automation and AI capabilities into the requirements for every role** ^[extracted] — AI fluency is now a hiring gate, not a nice-to-have.

**Compensation philosophy**: Geo-ranged global pay bands ("pay above in-location rates"), pay-equity framing, internal mobility as a stated culture pillar. Salary bands are deliberately wide because they span geographies and sometimes multiple career levels.

**Stage / scale** ^[inferred]: Late-stage, well-funded HR-tech scaleup (EOR is a competitive category — Deel, Oyster, Velocity Global, Papaya Global are peers). Exact funding stage / ARR not stated in the source JD; the maturity of the comp philosophy, ERG structure, and multi-layer interview process imply a mid-to-late-stage company of meaningful headcount, not an early scaleup.

## Notable details

- 4 ERGs (Women, Disability, Queer, Minorities in Tech); EEO employer with anonymous demographic data collection.
- Benefits: work-from-anywhere, flexible PTO, async hours, 16 weeks paid parental leave, stock options, learning budget, home-office + IT budget, co-working stipend.
- Published AI usage guidelines; embraces AI "while prioritizing human creativity and authenticity."
