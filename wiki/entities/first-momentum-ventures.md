# First Momentum Ventures

**Type**: Pre-seed venture capital fund
**Tagline**: "Engineers at heart, investors by choice" / "The companion for technical founders at hour zero"
**HQ**: Karlsruhe, Germany (with Munich presence)
**Founded**: 2017 (some sources cite 2019 as the "campus spin-off" reframing — they describe themselves as having lived pre-seed "since 2015")
**Origin**: Spun out of Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)
**Stage**: Pre-seed primary, occasional seed follow-on
**Geography**: Europe; primary focus DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland)
**Cheque size**: €250K–€1.5M as lead or co-invest (some sources cite €200K–€1M for Fund II first cheques)

## Funds and capital

- **Fund I** — earlier vintage, exact size not disclosed in public sources
- **Fund II** — €35M closed; backed by 40+ AI founders and operators acting as LPs
- **Fund III** — referenced as "on the horizon" in their 2026 Visiting Associate JD; not yet closed/announced ^[inferred from JD]
- **AUM**: ~€100M (per VC Beast)
- **Track record**: 42 portfolio companies total (Mar 2026); 7 new investments in trailing 12 months; 2 already in 2026 (Telura Energy, ISPTech). Portfolio claims €500M+ follow-on raised (one source claims €1B+ since 2018).

## Investment thesis and sectors

Backs "highly ambitious teams that address hard problems with complex technology." The differentiator is technical depth: partners with engineering and research backgrounds (KIT, MIT, Max Planck) claim to evaluate complex tech without needing founders to translate.

**Sectors:**
- Climate & energy
- Compute / AI & ML
- Software infrastructure / dev tools & data
- Manufacturing & robotics
- Deep tech (quantum, aerospace, novel materials)
- Computational biology
- Enterprise SaaS

**Portfolio shape** (sample):
- *Climate & energy*: Brineworks (DAC), Encosa (industrial storage), HeyCharge (EV charging), Hyperdrives (electric drives), Gauss Fusion
- *Deep tech / industrial*: Daedalus (autonomous factories), Dive (particle simulation), ISPTech (green spacecraft propulsion)
- *Enterprise software*: Cello (PLG referrals), Edyoucated (upskilling), Enneo (AI CS for energy), IMPARGO (TMS), Jam (AI sales training)
- *ML*: Atlas Bio (drug efficacy foundation model), ContextSDK (privacy-preserving mobile context)

Climate/energy and enterprise SaaS are the heaviest weightings. Few portfolio companies have reached Series A (Daedalus, Dive are exceptions); the rest sit pre-seed/seed.

## Differentiators

- **Engineer-led evaluation** — partners with technical depth claim to assess hard tech without translation overhead. This is the main marketing wedge.
- **Scout-network deal flow** — 100+ scouts embedded in European universities give early access to research spin-outs before they hit the conventional VC funnel.
- **AI founder/operator LP base** — Fund II is backed by 40+ AI founders and operators; signal value for portfolio companies seeking introductions.
- **"IQ = EQ" framing** — high-touch, family-style founder relationships positioned as competitive advantage vs traditional funds.
- **Equity participation across team** — every team member shares in fund economics (per their Visiting Associate JD).

## Competitive positioning (DACH pre-seed)

Operates in the same European pre-seed deep-tech tier as Cherry Ventures (broader thesis), Speedinvest (multi-stage, broader geography), HV Capital (later stage), and Earlybird (later stage). First Momentum's narrow wedge is **technical pre-seed in DACH** with a scout network as the deal-flow moat. Closest direct comparable: HTGF (deeper pockets, government-backed) and Atlantic Labs (broader thematic).
