# Elevantiq (formerly ALPIN11)

**Type**: Digital commerce agency / Vendure majority owner
**Founded**: 2019 (as ALPIN11)
**Rebranded**: October 2024 → Elevantiq
**HQ**: Kitzbühel + Vienna, Austria
**Tagline**: "Building enterprise software with open source at its core"
**Status (mid-2026)**: **Being shut down** — announced 2026-02-04 in the Vendure leadership letter. Team and capital consolidating into Vendure as a single-brand product company.

## Background

David Höck founded ALPIN11 in 2019 as an enterprise digital-commerce agency. Built B2B/B2C commerce systems for blue-chip clients including:
- **IBM** (Project Marlin — multi-tenant commerce)
- **Ruefa** (Austrian travel)
- **Breitling** (Swiss watches)

In 2022, ALPIN11 discovered Michael Bromley's open-source Vendure project, and after a series of conversations between Höck and Bromley they decided to build a company around it — ALPIN11 became a stakeholder and provided agency-billable hours to fund Vendure's development.

## October 2024 — Bootstrap inflection

After ~200 VC meetings, founders decided against outside funding. Instead, ALPIN11 acquired 80% of Vendure GmbH and rebranded to **Elevantiq** to reflect the joint identity. Michael Bromley became co-owner of Elevantiq alongside his Vendure CTO role; Max Page took COO across both entities; Mathias Lukas led sales/partnerships.

## Financial scale (as of Feb 2026 letter)

- **~€3M annual revenue** (Elevantiq)
- **Healthy margins** (profitable)
- **15+ FTE** (Elevantiq team)
- ~€800K of engineering time per year contributed directly to Vendure (instead of sold as consulting)

## February 2026 — Wind-down

The leadership letter announced that done-for-you agency work would stop. Reasons stated:
1. The agency competed with Vendure's partner network — bad for ecosystem growth
2. Optimising the agency P&L was "a local maximum" — couldn't 10x from there
3. The team's attention was split between billable hours and product

Replacement: "done-with-you" Professional Services — architecture review, training, expert support — bundled under Vendure brand. Not full project delivery.

## Why this matters

For anyone joining Vendure mid-2026, **Elevantiq is the answer to "where does the runway come from?"** Years of agency profits have been the funding source. The Feb 2026 decision to stop that flow is the most consequential strategic move in the company's history.
