# Research: ElevenLabs Growth Generalist — application angle for Mark

## Overview

The [[references/elevenlabs-growth-generalist-jd|Growth Generalist]] seat is the **horizontal floater on Luke Harries's Growth umbrella** at ElevenLabs, sitting next to four regional/product B2B marketers (NA Generalist, UK/I, ElevenCreative Lead, France launcher) and the Head of Performance Marketing (B2C/SMB). Its job is to **surge on the highest-priority projects** across that whole surface area — billboards at SFO, keynote prep for the Summit, influencer attribution dashboards — feeding [[entities/carles-reina|Carles Reina's]] enterprise sales motion ($500M+ ARR by May 2026, growing fast pre-IPO at a $11B valuation).

The role's operating shape **is** [[concepts/elevenlabs-no-titles-operating-model|the no-titles operating model in human form]]: no scope, no lane, output-judged. This is engineering-led culture — Luke Harries is ex-PostHog interim Head of Product / YC founder / Microsoft Research, not a marketing-pedigreed CMO. The hiring filter is operator-shape, not marketer-CV.

**Recommendation: apply.** Calibration: ~25-35% interview odds. Higher than Allocator One (10-15%), comparable to Vendure (25-40%). The seat is structurally better-aligned to Mark's actual shape than any other tracked role — it's the cleanest [[synthesis/mark-job-direction-map|frontier-lab GTM instance]] available, and it's a permanent commercial seat on the closest-to-his-baseline axis (vs FMV fixed-term or Allocator One investor-side).

## Key Findings

### 1. Company state — fast, big, IPO-track

- **$500M+ ARR by May 2026**, from $0 → $300M in 3 years; $350M end of 2025 → $450M end of Q1 2026 → $500M in April-May 2026 ([[entities/elevenlabs|company entity]]).
- **$11B valuation** after Series D Feb 2026 ($500M led by Sequoia, with Andrew Reed joining the board). A16Z + ICONIQ + Lightspeed + Bond + Evantic also in.
- **Targeting IPO-ready in 2-3 years** (Bloomberg, Mar 2026). Mati Staniszewski is openly considering a **dual Warsaw + London listing**.
- **41% of Fortune 500** use ElevenLabs (per SaaStr citing company).
- The seat is being hired into **pre-IPO commercial scale-up**, not founding-marketer phase.

### 2. The Growth Generalist seat in org context

Pulled from concurrent Ashby postings (snapshot 2026-06-09) — the **sibling roles** clarify the seat:

| Role | Wing | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Head of Performance Marketing** (open) | B2C / SMB | Performance channels, agency relations, build in-house team |
| **B2B Marketing - North America Generalist** (open) | B2B | Partner marketing + customer advocacy for ElevenAgents; PT/ET |
| **B2B Marketing - UK/I** (open) | B2B | UK/Ireland regional B2B |
| **B2B Marketing Lead - ElevenCreative** (open) | B2B | Marketing leadership for ElevenCreative product line |
| **Growth Marketer - France** (open) | B2B | France market launch |
| **Mobile Growth Manager** (open) | B2C | Mobile/app acquisition |
| **Growth Generalist** (open) | **Cross-team B2B** | **Global remote; surge on priorities; fill gaps** — this is Mark's seat |

So the Growth Generalist is **structurally a horizontal floater** — not regional, not product-line-locked, not channel-specific. The JD framing ("surge on the highest priority projects, and help fill the gaps as needed") is the org chart in role form.

### 3. The hiring filter is engineer-operator, not marketer-pedigree

The most load-bearing fact: **the head of the umbrella is [[entities/luke-harries|Luke Harries]]**, whose CV is Microsoft Research RL → YC HealthTech co-founder → PostHog interim Head of Product → ElevenLabs Head of Growth. He has never held a "Marketing Manager" title in his life. His public writing:

- "**Your first growth hire**" (Apr 2025) — generalists vs. specialists; effectively the spec for this seat.
- "**10x engineers talk to users**" (Mar 2023) — his version of the [[concepts/founding-gtm-engineer-thesis|engineer-seller thesis]].
- "**Why we've removed job titles at ElevenLabs**" (late 2024) — operating-model essay.
- Podcast: "AI tools that saved ElevenLabs **$100K+**" — AI-first is measurable, not slogan.

The Growth Generalist JD requirements (SQL + dashboards + spreadsheet modelling + copy + multi-project + tool-learning + creative + passion) is a near-direct translation of "the kind of person Luke Harries would hire if you gave him no titles to play with." This is **Mark's shape almost exactly**: [[skills/ai-powered-sales-tooling|three flagship AI sales tools]] (SQL + scripts + copy + dashboards) shipped end-to-end at [[entities/stackgini|Stackgini]] as the founding GTM hire.

### 4. The pipeline TAM frame

ElevenLabs's [[concepts/voice-ai-enterprise-tam|public enterprise TAM]]: "**$400B/yr on customer service; 10 hours on hold per UK resident per year**" (Mati's Feb 2026 London Summit keynote). The seat's mandate — "tens of millions of marketing spend → hundreds of millions of pipeline" — sits inside this wedge.

Confirmed enterprise deployments anchoring the pipeline story: **Revolut (4M customers, 8x faster), Klarna (35M US, 10x faster), Deutsche Telekom (network-embedded, 50 langs), Razorpay (Hinglish outbound), IBM (watsonx Orchestrate), Customers Bank, Harvey, Meta, Square, Salesforce, Ukrainian Government**.

The SFO airport billboard reference in the JD reads as **direct enterprise-buyer ad inventory** (CXO + IT-procurement audiences fly SFO en route to RSA / Money 20/20 / SaaStr), not consumer marketing.

### 5. The Summit + events surface is real and active

- **ElevenLabs Summit London Feb 2026**: 1000+ attendees at Magazine London, Mati keynote, enterprise panel with **BCG, Naturgy, Konecta**, Marco Mancini + AIUC on AI risk.
- Future Summits: London, Warsaw, San Francisco.
- London Tech Week 2026 exhibitor.
- First ElevenLabs billboard campaign (Luke Harries shared launch on LinkedIn).
- Voice-driven activation with H/L on AI Brock Purdy (12K+ interactions).
- Ambassador Program 2026 open.
- Customer Stories library at 88+.

This is **the actual day-to-day for the Growth Generalist**: keynote talking points for Summit speakers, billboard inventory negotiation for the SFO campaign and similar, influencer-attribution dashboards for the Ambassador Program. The JD examples are not hypotheticals — they're real ongoing work surfaces.

### 6. Compensation signals

Levels.fyi snapshot ([[entities/elevenlabs|see entity page]]): Software Engineer median UK £109K–£111K (London high £288K); Recruiter UK £63K–£92K; global median ~$128K. Equity 20-40% of total comp.

**The Growth Generalist band is not directly posted on Levels.fyi**, but it sits below Software Engineer band and above Recruiter band. Inferred range: **£70K-£110K base for a London-equivalent, plus equity grant calibrated to current $11B valuation**. ^[inferred] Remote-from-Vienna may face a Vienna-cost-of-living adjustment downward.

### 7. Location ambiguity — the one real risk

- JD says **"This role is remote and can be executed globally"**.
- Optional hubs listed: Bangalore, Dublin, London, New York, San Francisco, Tokyo, Warsaw. **No Vienna.**
- Company hires across 14+ EU countries; the explicit list found in research includes Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czechia, Denmark, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Spain, Sweden, UK — **Austria not explicitly listed**, but EMEA roles are open.
- Realistic read: **remote-from-Vienna is acceptable for the role itself** but B2B Marketing team gravity is in London (Summit, Tech Week, billboard campaigns, Luke based in UK) — meaningful travel expected if hired.

### 8. Application angle (operator vocabulary)

The differentiator vs typical Growth Generalist applicants (consultants pivoting, BD pedigreed, agency-side performance marketers):

1. **Engineer-seller proof, not claim.** [[skills/ai-powered-sales-tooling]] = three production tools built on raw LLM APIs — the SQL + scripts + copy + dashboards combination the JD asks for, *already shipped*. Match Luke's "10x engineers talk to users" vocabulary, not the demand-gen-funnel lexicon.
2. **Founding-GTM ownership at a B2B SaaS.** Cross-pillar fluency (positioning, demand-gen, sales-cycle ownership, fundraising-support) as a single operator at [[entities/stackgini]]. Few "Growth Generalist" candidates carry this depth.
3. **Pipeline-math literacy.** [[references/mark-education|CBS Financial Management]] + [[references/mark-prior-roles|Luther HGB Excel]] gives the modelling depth the JD asks for. Most marketing-pedigreed applicants lack this stack.
4. **Voice-AI enterprise-buyer instinct.** [[entities/stackgini|Stackgini's intra-IT buying-centre work]] ([[feedback_stackgini_buying_centers]]) maps directly onto how ElevenAgents lands in IT/CX procurement. Concrete enterprise-buyer instinct, not marketing-only instinct.
5. **Why-ElevenLabs narrative.** Possible angles:
   - The voice-AI surface is the part of the frontier where B2B procurement and consumer creative collide — an unusual GTM shape.
   - The $500M ARR / $400B TAM / 41% Fortune 500 framing makes ElevenLabs the company where engineer-seller execution compounds inside a frontier-lab brand with pipeline math at the centre, not a slogan.
   - Mark's existing AI-tooling work compounds inside a company that already operates AI-first as a measurable bar.

### 9. What NOT to do in the application

- **Don't pitch as a launchpad.** Culture rewards generalists who *want* the operator shape, not those treating it as transit to "Head of X."
- **Don't lead with marketing-funnel vocabulary** (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU, MQL/SQL, RevOps-speak). Use Luke's vocabulary: shipped, launch, talk to users, generalist.
- **Don't downplay the engineer track.** [[concepts/founding-gtm-engineer-thesis|Engineer-seller framing]] is the unfair advantage here, not a side note.
- **Don't apologise for the German/Austrian base.** The JD is "remote and can be executed globally"; the company hires in 30+ countries. Lean into it.
- **Don't write a generic AI-passion paragraph.** Cite a specific ElevenLabs product detail (Conversational AI 2.0 turn-taking, Eleven v3 expressiveness, Deutsche Telekom MWC embed, Klarna 10x speed) to prove product mastery.

## Contradictions & Open Questions

- **Location** — JD says "remote and can be executed globally" but explicit hubs exclude continental EU outside Warsaw + Dublin. Remote-from-Vienna acceptable, or does team gravity exclude it in practice? Surface explicitly in screen.
- **Comp band** — not posted; Levels.fyi has engineering bands but not Growth Generalist. Ask on screen.
- **Reporting line** — direct to Luke Harries, or to a B2B Marketing Lead under Luke? The org's "no titles" framing means this is genuinely unclear.
- **Which products the seat serves** — JD says "B2B Marketing team" but there's a separate ElevenCreative B2B Marketing Lead. The Growth Generalist likely serves all three (ElevenAgents, ElevenCreative, ElevenAPI) per the "surge" framing, but worth confirming.
- **Team headcount** — ~20 micro-teams of 5-10 ≈ 100-200 FTE; how many on B2B Marketing specifically not disclosed.
